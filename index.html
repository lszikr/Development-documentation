Background

This web design proposal aims to support and promote the chosen Australian charity and NGO: Conservation volunteers Australia, assisting the organisation to awaken individuals consciousness and take more awareness of the current causes to our environment. With the unification of all possible enthusiastic audiences being attracted to this event, we would really appreciate and encourage them to participate the campaign and therefore illuminate the light with us.

Research

For me as a successful Junior Web Designer at DesCo, I need to ensure our project is well incorporated with the exisiting website. Not only to fufill the user needs based on their motivations, and also take responsibilities of all the stakeholders and maximise the potential benefits. From what I have investigated, Conservation Volunteers had made a great number of contributions which could excite and engage the users. 'Conservation Volunteers was founded when discussion of environmental issues was fresh, vibrant and a touch radical. It was also a ‘different world’ from today: more Australians had a connection to the land; young people anticipated a career for life; private transport was less available; inbound tourism was in its infancy.'

User needs can be intriged by evoking a sense of compassion, "on the individual level, this mechanism posits that individuals enjoy helping per se and therefore do not need any external rewards, suchas material compensation or reciprocation, to beneﬁt fromthe action emotionally (Meier & Stutzer, 2004).

In conclusion, donation also plays an important role in this mechanism, "motives of gift giving being either altruistic (maximising the pleasure of the recipient, or agnoistic(maximising the donor's personal satisfaction)"(Sherry, 1988).

User Profiles

There are no limitations to the audience as it is a complete non-profit public welfare organisation, anyone who is excited in this event is welcome to participate and will get attracted to this earth protection event. We don't intend to force anyone to join, it is a spontaneous collaborative activity which we offer you the opportunity to you online. The main targeted group here is mainly teenagers who are interested to get benefits from it and eventually everyone will obtain the joyfulness, love and compassion and all other forms of abundance. With some of the middle aged and elderly people who are fascinated to include themselves and maybe share a little portion of their accumulated money to help in the acquisition of tools.

“What was started in 1982 by Yandoit farmer, Tim Cox, as a small group planting trees on weekends has become the leading practical conservation group in Australia, managing thousands of volunteers who help out on dozens of projects each year.” — Colin Jackson OAM, CEO 1988-2017, Conservation Volunteers

Ideation

The ideation process was accomplished with multiple refinements. The concepts are firstly drawn into low fidelity sketches. The icons and layout were brainstormed and roughly created to present a visualisation to illustrate the potential forms of how the website may look like. Then the method of using wireframes enables me to transform imaginated construction into a more refined and transit to a higher fidelity prototype. Then it finishes and exceed a final version of web design with mockups.
